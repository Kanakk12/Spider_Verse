Pavitra's Profile

Pavitra Prabhakar

Pavitr Prabhakar, a poor Indian boy, lived in a village, but moves to Mumbai with his aunt Maya and uncle Bhim to study after getting half a scholarship. His parents died some years earlier. Other boys at the school teased him and beat him. Thus Pavitr came to understand that with great power comes great responsibility.
